A Day in the Life

What it actually looks like to work on satellite-derived economic intelligence.

9:00 AM
Morning Standup
Quick async check-in across time zones. Teams share overnight model runs, flag anomalies in satellite feeds, and align on the day's priorities.
10:30 AM
Deep Work Block
Engineers refine ML pipelines. Economists validate forecast signals against incoming BEA data. Data scientists tune geospatial models on fresh imagery.
1:00 PM
Research Review
Cross-functional session where science and economics teams present findings. Recent topic: nighttime luminosity as a leading indicator for port activity.
3:30 PM
Client Intelligence Delivery
Weekly nowcast reports go out to 120+ institutional clients. The product team reviews dashboard feedback and prioritizes feature requests.

Our Hiring Process

A transparent, four-step process designed to respect your time and evaluate mutual fit.

Application

Submit your resume and a short note on why Atlas interests you. We review every application within a week.

Intro Call

A 30-minute conversation with our recruiting team to learn about your background and share more about the role.

Technical Round

A focused exercise relevant to the role. No trick questions, no whiteboard hazing. Real problems we actually solve.

Team Meet

Meet your future teammates. We want to make sure the fit is mutual. You'll get honest answers about what it's like here.
