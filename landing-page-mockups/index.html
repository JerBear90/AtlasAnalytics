Methodology

From Orbit to Insight

How we transform terabytes of satellite imagery into actionable economic intelligence.

Capture

We ingest daily satellite imagery from multiple orbital sources — optical, SAR, and nighttime luminosity — covering every major economy.

Process

Proprietary ML pipelines extract economic signals from raw imagery: port activity, construction patterns, agricultural output, urban mobility, and more.

Deliver

Signals are calibrated against macroeconomic models and delivered as GDP nowcasts, trade indicators, and component forecasts via API and dashboard.
