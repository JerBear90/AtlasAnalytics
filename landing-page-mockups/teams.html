Our Origin

Why We Built Atlas

Traditional economic data arrives weeks or months late — released on schedules designed decades ago. By the time GDP figures reach decision-makers, the economy has already moved. We founded Atlas Analytics to close that gap. By applying proprietary machine learning models to satellite imagery captured daily, we deliver macroeconomic intelligence in near real-time — giving investors, policymakers, and researchers the confidence to act before consensus forms. Today, our forecasts cover 38 countries and are trusted by over 120 institutional clients worldwide.
